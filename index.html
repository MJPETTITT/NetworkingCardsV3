GETTING TO KNOW EACH OTHER

The goal of this exercise is to meet people you haven’t met yet, and get to know them a little better in 3 easy steps:

1. Once you are paired with someone. Click the cards to reveal your two question options.

2. Ask you partner one of the two questions on your card.

3. Answer one of the two questions on their card.

Try to do this in less than one minute! There is a timer to help you keep track of time.

TOP TIPS

Be flexible: adapt your question if you notice it’s difficult to answer.

Pay attention to the time limit (60’’), so that you talk to as many people as possible.
